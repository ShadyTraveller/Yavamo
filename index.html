FAQs

Questions the booking flow answers clearly.

Can customers still call or email?

Yes. The website supports phone, email, and web bookings. Online is simply the fastest structured option.

Do customers need an account?

No. This version keeps things simple and avoids logins, dashboards, and account management.

How do cancellations work?

All cancellations and booking changes happen through email after confirmation. That keeps operations light while still giving customers a clear path.

Does online booking save the details somewhere?

Yes. When deployed with Supabase, bookings can be stored with service details, preferred date and time, address, and marketing attribution fields.
